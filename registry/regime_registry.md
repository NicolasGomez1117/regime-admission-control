Regime Registry

## Purpose
Define allowable `regime_id` values.

### SETTLEMENT_RAILS_INCIDENT
Semantics: Declares a regime context in which settlement rail conditions are explicitly in scope for observation and documentation. No rules or thresholds are implied.
Non-claims: Not admissibility criteria; not action authorization.

### STABLECOIN_PEG_EVENT

Non-claims: Not admissibility criteria; not action authorization.

Non-claims: Not admissibility criteria; not action authorization.
